Plant Facility

Team Members
BEST has recruited experience team members for all the machines, for correct and smooth functioning of the machines and dies and in turn meeting the production required.
Tool Setting
BEST believes that for any success of a stamping industry tool setting specialist is a must for maintaining tools and presses, BEST has recruited a tool setter having 25 years of experience in tool setting for all types of presses ranging from 60T to 300T.
Tool Maintenance
But not the least BEST has also has a tie for tooling support from a well established industries- Bangalore Tool Room having facilities of tool room-wire cut machines, CNC machines, surface and cylindrical grinding machines. BEST will use the facilities of Bangalore tool room to maintain the tools. BEST has a plan to maintain spares for tools which run in the shop floor for smooth and uninterrupted parts supply.
Tool Design
BEST will use the facilities of Bangalore tool room and in house expertise for tool design and development.
Bangalore tool room is one of the leading tool room in Bangalore, the company is specialist in precession tooling, specifically in carbide and HSS press tools we cater to wide industry segment namely Powder compaction, Bearing, connector, Electrical and Razor blade industries, our specialty is making tools free from burr.
